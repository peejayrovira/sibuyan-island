Sibuyan Island, Romblon

The Galapagos of Asia

It is a paradise that you will reach after a whole day journey by land and sea, could be longer. If you can wait long enough, if you can endure the the pain and uncomfortable situations, and if you can brave the dusts of rough roads and sometimes unfriendly waves of the sea; only then you can lay your eyes to this charming and enchanting paradise.
